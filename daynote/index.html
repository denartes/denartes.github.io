What is Daynote?

Daynote is designed to help you stay organised without unnecessary complexity. It combines task tracking, routine planning, and simple scheduling into a clean, distraction-free experience.

Designed for clarity

Instead of overwhelming dashboards and cluttered views, Daynote focuses on giving you a clear understanding of what needs to be done, when, and why.

Private by default

Your data stays on your device. Optional Google Drive sync is available if you want backup and cross-device access, but nothing is stored on developer-controlled servers.
