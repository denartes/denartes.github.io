Daynote Privacy Policy

Daynote is a personal productivity app.

Data Collection

Daynote may use your Google account email address for authentication and sync functionality. This data is not shared with third parties by the developer.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device or in your connected Google account services.

Data Deletion

You can remove your data at any time by disconnecting your Google account in the app or uninstalling the app.
