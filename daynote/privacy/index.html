Daynote Privacy Policy

Daynote is a personal productivity app.

Data Collection

Daynote allows users to optionally sync their data with their Google Drive. When using this feature, the email address associated with the Google account may be stored locally on the device for authentication and app functionality. This data is not shared with third parties by the developer.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device or in your connected Google account services.

Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time by disconnecting your Google account in the app, uninstalling the app, or deleting your data directly from your Google Drive.
