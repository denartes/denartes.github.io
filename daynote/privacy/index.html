Daynote Privacy Policy

Daynote is a personal productivity app.

Data Collection

Daynote does not collect any personal data unless the user chooses to enable Google sync. When Google sync is enabled, the email address associated with the user's Google account is stored locally on the device.

Data Usage

The stored email address is used solely for display and to to support authentication with Google and syncing app data with the user's Google Drive. This data is not shared with third parties by the developer.

Data Storage

The email address is stored only on the user's device. Daynote does not store personal data on servers controlled by the developer.

Data Deletion

The stored email address can be removed at any time by disconnecting the Google account within the app, clearing the app data on the device, or by uninstalling the app.
